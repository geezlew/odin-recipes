The Ulimate Carbonara

An easy-cook tasty Carbonara!

A quick and tasty recipe for carbonara without relying on unhealthy pre-bought sauces. With this recipe you can easily make carbonara from scratch - just don't scramble those eggs at the end!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • 3 Eggs
  • As much Cheddar and Parmesan as you can handle
  • Mushrooms
  • Enough spinach to make you think this is healthy
  • Garlic Bread of course

Steps

  1. Boil water and add spaghetti
  2. Fry up onion and garlic and once these soften, mushrooms
  3. Beat 3 eggs with parmesan, cheddar and seasoning in a jug
  4. Add cooked spaghetti, spinach and eggs to the onion, garlic and mushrooms - fry on a low heat to prevent eggs scrambling.
  5. Serve with Garlic bread
