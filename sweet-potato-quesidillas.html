Sweet Potato Quesadillas

A delicious quick-cook quesadilla for two!

This recipe is perfect for when you're strapped for time and have limited ingredients in the cupboard. Despite all the cheese, the quantities of spinach and sweet potato make it taste almost healthy!

Ingredients

  • 2 Sweet Potatoes
  • 400g Green lentils
  • 1 Red Onion
  • An obscene amount of cheddar cheese
  • Enough spinach to make you think this is healthy
  • 4 wraps

Steps

  1. Cut and boil the potatoes
  2. Fry up onion and garlic
  3. Once all cooked, drain potatoes and mash together. Add cumin and season to taste
  4. Add potatoes to a wrap with cheese, spinach and top with another wrap. Warm until cheese has melted - serve.
